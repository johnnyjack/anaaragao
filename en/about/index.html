About

Ana Aragão (Porto, 1984) is an architect. She lives and works in Porto.

It is from the universe of architecture that her fascination for the portrayal of cities is born, however real or imaginary they may be.

Illustrator? Painter? Ana prefers simply say that she draws, and she draws a lot.

Constatly intrigued by the mental and emotional maps that arise from everyday experience, she explores that obsession through the finest lines, or through a palette full of colors. The city is, after all, not about its buildings, but about the people who live (in) there. The city is, after all, just the pretext for human.


Her clients include JP Sá Couto, Porto Barros, Schmidt Light Metal, among others.
